Our Programs & Activities

Driving change through dedicated spiritual, educational, and medical initiatives.

A Calendar of Compassion

Throughout the year, Ramnagar Sahayogi organizes and participates in numerous events designed to uplift the community. Our initiatives range from providing immediate medical relief through Ayurvedic camps to nurturing long-term spiritual growth through grand festivals and temple visits.

Key Initiatives

14-15 April 2024

Free Ayurveda Medical Camp

Organized at Bahiri, Kalindi, and Local Board Gosalas, our expert team of Ayurvedic doctors provided free consultations and medicine to over 230 patients over two days.

16 August 2025

Program at Ranabagpur

A grand upcoming celebration marking the Incarnation of Babaji by Jogna and the Janmashtami of Bhagaban Krishna at Ramgopal Majumder Place, drawing devotees from far and wide.

07 January 2024

Visit to Swaminarayan Mandir

Our managing team visited the prestigious Swaminarayan Mandir in Kolkata, meeting with the head Swamiji to discuss spiritual collaborations and future community projects.

2024

Janmashtami at Jadavpur

A joyous and grand celebration of Bhagaban Krishna's Janmashtami with our trust members, spreading devotion, music, and community spirit throughout the local area.

01 January 2025

Bahiri Gosala Visit

A dedicated visit to the Bahiri Gosala to support animal welfare, oversee the care and nourishment of the cows, and uphold our commitment to serving all living beings.

2025

Gurukul Project Meeting

A strategic leadership team meeting held near Ruby, Kolkata to discuss the architectural and educational blueprint for the upcoming Gurukul Educational Institute.

Ramnagar Sahayogi

A charitable trust dedicated to fostering health, education, and spiritual well-being. Guided by the principle that service to humanity is service to God, we strive to build a more compassionate world.
